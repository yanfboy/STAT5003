For a method such as linear regression, it is particularly susceptible to outliers. As the model tries to reduce the error of the outlier this may result in poorer predictions for the non-outliers. To assess the impact we can compare the R-square of the model before and after the removal of the outliers. This can help us gauge the deterioration in the mdoel due to outliers
